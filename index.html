about us

Every day is a chance to become better

Shark Fitness Center is a well known gym which provides 24/7 service to the residents of Jaffna to help people achieve their fitness related goals. At our Shark Fitness Center you'll find the best set of fitness equipments and well trained Instructors. You can experience the latest weight lifting equipments and cardio machines at our gym which will help your motive of being healthy. Our entire staff and Instructors are committed to provide our members with the best fitness experience.


"Our vision is to serve the people with sound fitness knowledge and techniques such that members of our gym can improve their health and build self-confidence that is required to enhance the quality of their life".


"Our mission is to train the fitness enthusiast so that they can become stronger and productive".
